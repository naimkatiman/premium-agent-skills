# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct that could reasonably
be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers
via a GitHub issue or direct contact. Maintainers will review and respond, and
may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
